Diego Armando Maradona

The Golden Boy

Here is a little time-line of Diego Armando Maradona

  • 1960 - Born in Lanus, Argentina
  • 1969 - started his youth career with Argentinos Juniors
  • 1976 - started his senior football career with Argentinos Juniors, having 166 appearances and scoring 116 goals
  • 1981 - He signed for Boca Juniors, having 40 appearances and scoring 28 goals. He also won the league title during his time with them.
  • 1977 - Maradona played his first World Cup tournament in 1982 in his new country of residence, Spain. Argentina played Belgium in the opening game of the 1982 Cup at the Camp Nou in Barcelona.
  • 1984 - He signed for Napoli. He won many trophies for them, scoring 81 goals in 188 appearances
  • 1995 - He went back to his boy-hood club Boca Juniors, played for two years before retirement.
  • 1996 - He started his coaching career with Racing Club, goimg ahead to manage different football club even his national team Argentina.
  • 2020 - dies at the age of 60.
